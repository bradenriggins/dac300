Networking Tips

Start Early

Begin building relationships during your externship. These connections can lead to job opportunities and professional references.

Be Professional

Always maintain a professional demeanor in all interactions. Your reputation in the dental community matters.

Follow Up

Send thank you notes and follow up on conversations. A simple gesture can leave a lasting positive impression.

Stay Connected

Use LinkedIn to maintain professional connections. Share relevant articles and engage with your network's posts.

Ask Questions

Show genuine interest in others' experiences. People appreciate when you value their insights and expertise.

Give Back

Networking is a two-way street. Look for opportunities to help others in your network as well.
